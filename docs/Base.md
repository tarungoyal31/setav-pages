# Setav
Setav is a platform that connects people through cards.

There are two types of users on Setav:
1. Individuals: People who want to create and share digital cards. They can create a personal digital card that can be 
shared with others by scanning a QR code or clicking a link.
Individual cards are private and can be shared through the one time links. 
These cards typically includes personal information such as name, contact details, social media links, and other relevant information.
2. Businesses: Professionals who want to sell their services through digital cards. They can create business cards that showcase
their services, prices, and contact information. These services can be purchased directly through the card
Business cards are public and can be discovered by anyone browsing the platform.

Setav aims to provide a simple and efficient way for individuals and businesses to connect and share information digitally.

## Features
- Create and share digital cards
- QR code generation for easy sharing
- One-time links for private sharing
- Service listing and purchasing for business cards

### Business specific features
- Service catalog with pricing
- Secure payment processing
- Booking a time slot directly through the card. Calendar invite is sent to both parties upon booking confirmation.
